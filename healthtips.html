Tips For a Healthy Lifestyle

  1. Strive to consume at least 8 cups (64 ounces) of water per day, or more if you're physically active or in a hot environment.
  2. Consume a variety of nutritious foods to support immune function, energy levels, and mental well-being.
  3. Practice meditation for at least 10 minutes per day to reduce stress and improve mental well-being.
  4. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic exercise per week
  5. Take breaks from sitting and incorporate movement into your day to reduce the negative effects of prolonged sitting, such as obesity and poor posture
